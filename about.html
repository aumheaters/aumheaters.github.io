About Us

Description

Aum Heaters is engaged in Manufacturing all Kinds of Industrial Heaters, Thermocouples & Sensors along with Special Insulated Heater Jackets. We are Providing our Hi-Efficient Products to many OEM's & Leading End Users across India.
We provide Ceramic, Mica, Tubular ,Cast-in , oil ,Cartridge Heaters and All Kinds of Thermocouples & Sensors along with Aeroflex Micro porous Technology Insulated Heater Jackets which provide power savings upto 40 %.

All Heaters are manufactured using the Best Raw Materials available Like :-

  • Nichrome 80-20 Wires
  • SS Braided Lead Wires
  • Aluminized Steel Covers
  • Seamless Steel & Incloy Pipes
  • Best MGO , Natural Mica etc. on Latest Machines by our Skilled Technicians

Heaters are Tested as per International Standards.

We provide Technical Support in solving Heating related Issues with Proper Wattages required for different Applications which inturn Helps in Best Performance ,Power Savings & Optimum Output of Processing Machines.

Quality , Innovation , Technical Excellence & Fast Delivery are the services we offer you.
